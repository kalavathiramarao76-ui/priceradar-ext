Detailed Description

PriceHawk is an AI-powered price tracker that helps you make smarter purchasing decisions. Get instant price analysis, compare alternatives, predict price drops, and know exactly when to buy or wait.


Price Analysis: Understand the full market picture with price range data, trend analysis, and a value score (1-10) for any product.


Product Comparison: Compare multiple products side-by-side with detailed feature and price comparison tables.


Dynamic Pricing: Analyze price volatility, demand patterns, and get 30-day price predictions to time your purchases.


Alert Strategy: Set 3 custom price thresholds with AI-powered drop likelihood analysis at 30, 60, and 90 day intervals.


E-Commerce Integration: Works on Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Best Buy, and Shopify stores with an injected analysis button on product pages.


Query History: Keep your last 10 price analyses for reference and tracking.
